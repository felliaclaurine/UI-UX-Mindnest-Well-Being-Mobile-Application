# MindNest: Mental Well-Being Mobile Application

MindNest is a mobile application designed to support teenagers' mental well-being through mood journaling, emotion tracking, relaxation activities, mental health assessments, and access to professional consultation.

## My Role
I was part of the design team as a **UI/UX Designer**, working under the 
Design Lead. My contributions included :

- **Prototyping** – assisted in creating interactive prototypes in Figma
- **MindBot (Chatbot Feature)** – designed the conversational AI interface for mood support and guidance
- **Profile Page** – designed user profile screens and settings
- **Pro-Connect Feature** – designed the end-to-end flow for connecting users with professional psychologists, including:
  - Psychologist detail (about, approach, rating, etc.)
  - Booking menu and schedule selection flow
- **Calm Space** – contributed to the design of the relaxation/calming activity feature

## Aligned with SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being
MindNest leverages technology to promote mental health awareness and emotional resilience among adolescents.

## Skills
Figma, Wireframing, UI Design, User Flow Design
